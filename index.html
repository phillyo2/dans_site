Meet the Team

Founder: Michael Benjamin was born a Floridian but raised most of his life in the Carolinas. After ten years working in the shotcrete business with his father, he has mastered the step by step process of pool building and has begun doing it on his own. Michael is a firm believer in building things to last. There is no such thing as skimping on steel or concrete when Michael builds a pool or retaining wall. Deep concrete footings and thick concrete walls are what allow Michael and our customers to sleep soundly at night.
Salesman/designer: Dan Giordano was born and raised in New York but has been settled in Charlotte since 2010. Dan has a Bachelor of Landscape Architecture degree from Clemson University and a deep desire to design and build. His passion for site design, vision for material combinations, and perception of comfortable spatial relationships is what makes him the ideal designer for your outdoor space. The difference between Dan and the average pool salesman is that he is in the hole getting his hands dirty every day. Not only does he know how to design, but he knows how to build what he puts on paper. Knowledge of both is so important for communication between the design studio and the job site.
Michael and Dan have joined their expertise in Construction and Design to be your trusted pool builder. Contact Us.
